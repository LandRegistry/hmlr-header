Record contractual control rights for land

You need to tell us about the rights your clients have over land in England and Wales. Some rights are exempt from being recorded.

What you need to do

You will need to provide details of the agreement including:

  • grantee
  • grantor
  • title numbers
  • contractual control rights

This service is also available in Welsh.

Before you start

You will need the signed contract to record the rights.

Read guidance about how to record contractual control rights.
